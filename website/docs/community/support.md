### Builder — $5/month

For people actively building along.

For people who want to help shape what Dilder becomes.

## Why Patreon?

This project takes real time — research, documentation, 3D printing iterations, firmware development, and running the community. Patreon support directly funds:

- Component purchases for prototype iterations
- 3D printing filament
- Domain and hosting costs
- Time spent writing the documentation and build posts

All code, documentation, and STL files are eventually made public for free. Patreon supporters just get them first.
