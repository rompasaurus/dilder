## Notes

This is a standard JST PH 2.0mm pitch connector — the most common LiPo battery connector in hobby electronics. The InnCraft Energy 1000mAh battery used in the breadboard prototype has a Molex 1.25mm connector, but JST PH 2.0mm is specified on the PCB for broader battery compatibility.

| Pin | Function | Wire Color |
|-----|----------|------------|
| 1 | Battery + | Red |
| 2 | Battery - | Black |
