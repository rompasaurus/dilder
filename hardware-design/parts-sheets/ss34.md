| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|

| Forward voltage drop | 0.55V typical @ 3A |
| Reverse leakage | 0.5mA @ 40V |
| Surge current | 80A (8.3ms pulse) |

## How It's Used

The SS34 sits between USB VBUS (5V) and the battery charge input. When USB is plugged in, the diode conducts and USB power feeds the charger and system. When USB is removed, the diode blocks reverse current from the battery flowing back toward the USB port.

This is the same role as the Schottky diode on the Raspberry Pi Pico between VBUS and VSYS.
