## How It Works

The FS8205A contains two N-channel MOSFETs connected back-to-back (common drain). The DW01A controls each gate independently:

- **MOSFET 1 (OD gate):** Controls discharge path — DW01A turns this off during over-discharge
- **MOSFET 2 (OC gate):** Controls charge path — DW01A turns this off during over-charge

This back-to-back arrangement allows independent control of charge and discharge current paths through a single component.

## Pin Connections

| Pin | Function | Connect To |
|-----|----------|------------|
| 1 | Source 1 | DW01A CS pin (current sense) + circuit GND |
| 2 | Gate 1 | DW01A OD pin (over-discharge control) |
| 3 | Source 2 | Battery negative terminal |
| 4 | Drain (common) | Internal (shared between both MOSFETs) |
| 5 | Gate 2 | DW01A OC pin (over-charge control) |
| 6 | Source 1 | Same as pin 1 (source of MOSFET 1) |
